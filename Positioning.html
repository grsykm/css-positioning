Static = Default Positioning

I am the default positioning. You don't usually have to input me specifically as I will already be in place unless changed.

Absolute

I am positioned to the viewport or window. I am not the easiest positioning div to work with but I can be handy when I need to be set in a specific place and not move around!

Fixed Positioning

I am fixed, even if you scroll, I am positioned relative to the accesskey="viewport or window". I am ideal for design elements such as a search bar or shopping cart as I will stay within the user's sight as they navigate the site.


Relative Positioning

I am positioned relative to where I am usually placed. I am an easier version of absolute and more flesible to work with.
